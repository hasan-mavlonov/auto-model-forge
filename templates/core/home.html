FAQ

Quick answers to the common questions we get.

How many images do I need?

We work best with 10–60 images. Use varied angles, lighting, and backgrounds for the most robust LoRA.

How long does training take?

Most jobs finish within ~24 hours after payment confirmation. We update your status on the job page.

How do I pay?

Scan the Alipay QR we provide and include your reference AMF-XXXX so we can verify quickly.

Can I get a refund if the model fails?

If we can't deliver a working model, we refund you. We're hands-on and monitor every job.

What formats do you deliver?

You receive a LoRA or full model file download link directly in your job detail page once completed.
